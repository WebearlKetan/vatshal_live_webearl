The typical timeline is as follow:

You contact us with your information and the services you would like.

After the in-person estimate with a member of our staff, they will send you an estimate via email. Depending on the type of work you are requesting, the estimate may be sent out the same day as the in-person estimate, or a few days later if there is some creativity involved.
